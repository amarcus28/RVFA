# State of the RVFA - Gameweek 37

## Premier League

Kade is closing in on a title in his first season, giving up 2 points on Shea last week to maintain a 56 point gap going into the final two rounds. In the battle for 3rd, Ryan had a strong showing to pull ahead of me by 12 points.

At the other end, Joe has opened up a 17 point lead over Mitchell for safety from the drop, though Mitchell could still turn this on its head if he manages to win the FA cup. Rob is just 24 points behind Mitchell, while Ryno looks all but set to go down this year.

## Championship

Not much has changed in the Championship table since the last update. Evan's lead has grown to 25 points over Mitch. Braxton and Leo still remain in the hunt 64 and 80 points behind Mitch respectively, though it looks increasingly likely they'll need to rely on the cup for promotion this season.

## RVFA Cup

Premier League clubs came out on top in the quarterfinals, knocking out the 3 remaining Championship owners. Several of the matchups were tightly contested. Mitchell retained his name, beating Mitch by just 3 points. Despite a 4 point hit, Alex used his triple captain chip to beat Evan by 1 point.

Ricky advanced over me on a goals scored tie breaker with a late Cherki assist off the bench to break my heart and end my chance at silverware this season. Winning this competition is Mitchell's chance to guarantee safety from the drop, and put Joe on the hot seat pending Championship cup results.

**Semifinal matchups:** Ricky vs. Ryan and Mitchell vs. Alex (Battle of the Board)

## Premier League Cup

The Premier League cup featured two close matchups in the quarterfinal round: Reece beating Mitchell by 1 point, and the same matchup of Ricky and myself that ended on goals scored tiebreaker. Strong performances from Joe and Shea see them progress as well.

**Semifinal matchups:** Ricky vs. Shea and Reece vs. Joe

## Championship Cup (aka Promotion Playoff)

Gabe, Cam, Jack, and Wes saw their promotion hopes fade in the quarterfinals, each beaten by a club currently in the top 5 of the division table. The closest matchup between Cam and Evan had just 6 points between them, while the remaining ties were all double digit victories.

Will Evan take the glory and guarantee only 2 promotions and relegations, or will another manager claim a third promotion spot this offseason?

**Semifinal matchups:** Evan vs. Jamie and Leo vs. Braxton
